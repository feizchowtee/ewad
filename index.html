About Us

We empower and engage children with disabilities for a brighter future.

Since its creation, EWAD has been operating in a rental building at Bel Air Riviere Seche, an easily accessible village for the children to provide proper care and attention to children with intellectual, physical and other disabilities.

Our Expertise

Because we genuinely care.

Too often, children with disabilities are defined and judged by what they lack rather than what they have. Their exclusion and invisibility from society serves to render them uniquely vulnerable, denying them respect, dignity and individuality. For those reasons, EWAD offers a wide range of services to boost your child’s health and ability to confront their routine notably:

Qualified Teachers

Qualified special needs teachers and assistance to help your child in different educational and creative aspects.

Professional Therapy

Paramedical specialists, such as speech therapists, physiotherapists, psychologists and occupational therapists. All four which are essential in helping your child to progress in their speech, psychological and physical skills.

Sports & Yoga Programs

Sport programs as well as regular training in physical education and sport competitions with the Special Olympics as well as weekly yoga classes with a specialised instructress to guide your child spiritually.

Professional Therapy

Transport facilities, daily pick up and drop off from your child’s domicile to our institution and drop off from our school to your child’s domicile after school hours.
